BITRE road safety enforcement

Police enforcement, told as a public-interest story.

This site is designed as a narrative data experience: each dataset has its own tab, its own analytical question, and its own chart sequence. The first live tab covers fines; the remaining tabs are scaffolded so future datasets can drop into the same structure without reworking the interface.

Why it matters

Enforcement data is not just administrative reporting. It signals where risky behaviour is being detected, how monitoring changes over time, and where collection practices still need harmonisation.
