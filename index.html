BITRE road safety enforcement

Police enforcement data shows where risky behaviour is being found, punished, and monitored.

The public record is uneven, but it is still revealing. It shows where speeding dominates the enforcement picture, where camera-led detection has changed the scale of recorded offences, and where cross-jurisdiction comparisons need caution because the underlying systems do not line up perfectly.

Why it matters

Enforcement data does not tell the whole road-safety story, but it does reveal what authorities are measuring, what technology they rely on, and which risky behaviours remain highly visible in the system.
